Learning Disability Support in Gorakhpur

Bright Horizon Learning Center provides specialized education for children who struggle in traditional school settings. Our certified special educators use multi-sensory teaching methods to help children succeed.

We help children with:

  • Dyslexia - Reading difficulties, letter reversals
  • Dyscalculia - Math difficulties, number sense problems
  • Dysgraphia - Writing difficulties, poor handwriting
  • Slow Learners - Below average academic performance
  • Academic Delays - Behind grade level in subjects
  • School Readiness - Not ready for mainstream school

Our Special Education Approach

We use multi-sensory, individualized teaching methods that work with your child's learning style:

  • Orton-Gillingham approach for reading
  • Hands-on math manipulatives
  • Visual learning aids and graphic organizers
  • Individualized Education Plans (IEPs)
  • Study skills and memory strategies
  • School coordination and advocacy
