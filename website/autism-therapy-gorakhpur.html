Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Treatment in Gorakhpur

Bright Horizon Learning Center provides comprehensive autism therapy services in Gorakhpur. As an AIIMS Gorakhpur referral partner, we offer evidence-based interventions for children with:

  • Mild to Moderate Autism - Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • Social Communication Disorder - Difficulty with social interactions
  • Pragmatic Language Disorder - Trouble using language socially
  • Peer Interaction Difficulties - Challenges making friends
  • Repetitive Behaviors - Stimming and routines
  • Sensory Processing Issues - Over or under-sensitivity

Our Autism Therapy Approach

We use a multi-disciplinary approach combining speech therapy, occupational therapy, and special education to address all aspects of your child's development:

  • Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) techniques
  • Social skills group training
  • Visual supports and structured teaching
  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Parent training and home strategies
  • School coordination and support
