ADHD Therapy & Treatment Center in Gorakhpur

Bright Horizon Learning Center offers comprehensive ADHD treatment in Gorakhpur. We help children with attention difficulties learn to focus, complete tasks, and succeed in school and at home.

Our program addresses:

  • ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder)
  • Attention Deficit Disorder (ADD) - Inattentive type
  • Hyperactivity - Can't sit still, always on the go
  • Impulsivity - Acts without thinking
  • Sensory Processing Issues - Over or under-responsive
  • Executive Function Difficulties - Organization, planning

Our ADHD Treatment Approach

We use a combination of occupational therapy, behavioral strategies, and special education techniques:

  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Attention training exercises
  • Impulse control strategies
  • Organizational skills training
  • Movement breaks and sensory diets
  • Parent coaching for home management
