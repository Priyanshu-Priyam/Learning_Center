Answers to questions parents frequently ask us.

No formal diagnosis is required. If you have concerns about your child's development, that's enough to start. Our assessment will help identify the right program for your child.

Our free consultation and comprehensive assessment will guide the recommendation. Our experts will evaluate your child and suggest the best-fit program based on their unique needs and goals.

We're trained to engage reluctant learners using play-based, child-centered approaches. Most children who initially resist come to enjoy their sessions within a few weeks as they build trust with our therapists.

Regular tuition teaches curriculum content. Our programs are therapy-integrated and individualized ‚Äî we address the underlying developmental or processing challenges that make learning difficult. We use specialized techniques, not just teaching methods.
