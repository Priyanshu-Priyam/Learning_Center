## Program Summaries

### 1. Little Talkers
**"Every child has a voice waiting to be heard"**

For children aged 2-4 years with speech delays, limited vocabulary, or unclear speech. This program builds foundational communication skills from first words to simple sentences through play-based speech therapy, sensory activities, and parent-guided home practice.

**Key Outcomes:** 50+ functional words, 2-3 word phrases, improved speech clarity

---

### 2. Ready, Set, School!
**"Building confident learners, one step at a time"**

For children aged 4-6 years who need support preparing for mainstream schooling. This comprehensive program develops pre-academic skills, fine motor abilities, social readiness, and self-help skills through structured classroom-like activities.

**Key Outcomes:** School readiness, 15+ min attention span, basic literacy & numeracy, self-care independence

---

### 3. Focus Champions
**"Harnessing energy into achievement"**

For children aged 5-8 years with ADHD, attention difficulties, or hyperactivity. This program uses sensory strategies, organizational tools, and behavioral techniques to help children channel their energy productively and succeed in academic settings.

**Key Outcomes:** Improved attention span (50-100%), impulse control, task completion, emotional regulation

---

### 4. Social Stars
**"Making friends, building connections"**

For children aged 4-7 years with autism (mild-moderate) or social communication challenges. Through structured social skills training, group activities, and real-world practice, children learn to connect with peers and navigate social situations confidently.

**Key Outcomes:** Eye contact, turn-taking, emotion recognition, cooperative play, emerging friendships

---

### 5. Learning Launchpad
**"Unlocking every child's learning potential"**

For children aged 6-10 years with learning difficulties, dyslexia indicators, or academic struggles. Using multi-sensory teaching methods, this program builds strong foundations in reading, writing, and math while developing effective study strategies.

**Key Outcomes:** Phonics-based reading, basic math operations, legible handwriting, study skills, academic confidence

## Our Approach

### Structured 6-Month Journeys

Each program follows a carefully designed 6-month curriculum with:
- **Monthly themes** that build progressively
- **Weekly milestones** for tracking progress
- **Daily activities** reinforced at home
- **Regular assessments** to measure growth

### Parent Partnership Model

Parents are essential partners in their child's development:
- **Weekly training sessions** to learn strategies
- **Daily home practice** with activity cards
- **Monthly progress meetings** with detailed reports
- **Video documentation** to see visible growth

### Integrated Therapy Approach

Our programs blend three core disciplines:
- **Speech Therapy:** Communication, language, articulation
- **Occupational Therapy:** Fine motor, sensory, daily living skills
- **Special Education:** Academic readiness, cognitive skills, behavior
