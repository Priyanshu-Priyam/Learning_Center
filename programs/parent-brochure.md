## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does my child need a diagnosis?**
No diagnosis is required. If you have concerns, that's enough to start.

**Q: How do I know which program is right?**
Our free consultation and assessment will guide the recommendation.

**Q: What if my child doesn't cooperate?**
We're trained to engage reluctant learners. Most children enjoy sessions within weeks.

**Q: Will this cure my child's condition?**
We focus on skill-building and progress, not "cures." Every child can learn and grow.

**Q: Do you coordinate with my child's school?**
Yes, we provide reports and strategies for teachers (with your permission).

**Q: What if 6 months isn't enough?**
Many children continue with extended or maintenance programs. We plan together.

**Q: Is this like therapy or tuition?**
It's a blend ‚Äî therapy-based techniques applied to learning and skill development.
