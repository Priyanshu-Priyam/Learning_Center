## Image Guidelines
- Format: PNG (with transparency) or JPG
- Resolution: 72-150 DPI for screen, 300 DPI for print
- Max file size: 500KB per image for fast loading
- Color space: sRGB

---
These images are optional - the booklet works with emoji icons by default.
